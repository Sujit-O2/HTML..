Nalanda Institute of Technology Bhubaneswar, a brain child of Balaji Educational Trust has come out of its cocoon in the year 2006 with an objective to impart value & quality based technical education not only to the students of Orissa but also to the students of eastern India. This state of the art institute is laid out in over 32 acres at Chandaka square, Bhubaneswar and approved by AICTE, New Delhi, affiliated to BPUT & recognized by Govt. of Orissa. The Management hopes to render yeoman service to the student folks to facilitate them to pursue a career options. With an experience of 8 years at the backdrop in running technical institutions, we know the art of teaching better than others. NIT, Bhubaneswar will focus on quality and rigorous education, academic infrastructure, technology, research and innovation. The institute is committed to the entire value chain of knowledge creation, diffusion and preservation to meet the challenges of the century. The institute will borrow best practices in education delivery systems, research and consulting practice. The institute will cultivate values of Honesty and Transparency, Respect for the individuals, Commitment to quality and high standards, Passion for performance and sensitivity to social and ethical issues. Nalanda Institute of Technology reaffirms its pledge to provide quality education by imparting value & quality based teaching to the students of eastern India in general & the students of Orissa in particular and facilitates the students to materialize the Dreamz Unlimited.
